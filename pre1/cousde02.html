Scouting Report
Great shooting touch... Feathery touch out to 12-15 feet... Good free throw shooter, but should look to get to the line more often... Good passer, has the ability to make the necessary pass from the low post... Above average ball skills for his height... Attacks the rim well with excellent leaping ability and body control... Competitor who does all the dirty work inside... Long arms help with rebounding and make him a good shot blocker... Truly a freak of nature in terms of athletic ability, agility and mobility, he simple has the ability to do anything and everything on the basketball court that he wants to do

Decision making is not always great
