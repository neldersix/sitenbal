Scouting Report
Has shown the ability to create his own shot off the dribble and pulls up quickly and efficiently, squares to the basket very well and gets vertical... Quality free throw shooter... He has a scorers mentality, but also passes the ball well especially on the dribble drive... Plays sound defense and his foot speed can create problems for opponents... Can get anywhere on the floor whenever he wants

Needs to improve upon his outside shot, which is solid but can improve... Must become a better rebounder, especially on the defensive side
