Scouting Report
Has excellent touch within 12 feet of the basket... Has decent ball handling ability and even likes to take the ball coast to caost, but his future is as a post man... A scrappy player who loves to hit the glass hard on both ends of the floor... Excellent on defense with great foot speed and the potential to become a lock down defender... He is a decent shot-blocker, but does position himself well on defense... He is not too aggressive but not afraid either... Outstanding stamina

Shot has gone cold in late season... Also needs to improve from the free throw line... Struggles to make easy passes with defenders collapsing his passing lanes
