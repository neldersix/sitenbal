Scouting Report
Can face the basket from 12-15 feet and get by bigger players off the dribble as well as knock down shots out to 18 feet with consistency... Free throw shooting fell off some lately but is solid... Excellent vision and passing skills... He can pass and plays tough defense when he wants to... Makes good decisions and finds shots within the flow of the game, looking to get everyone involved and shooting when necessary... One of the most athletic players in all of pro basketball

Extremely streaky shooter, who if he's on can single handedly win the game, but who can also go scoreless for 20 minutes at a time... If he adds additional strength, he will be able to fight for rebounds better
