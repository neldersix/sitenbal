Scouting Report
His greatest strength is shooting. Has a very soft touch... Excellent body control, can get some spectacular ally oops and put backs when crashing the glass... Good free throw shooter... He will immediately be one of the best passing big men in the country, whether it’s out of the post, double team, in the open court, or on the perimeter.... Unlike most players his size, he can put the ball on the floor and shows tremendous court vision and a nice passing touch... Loves to bang, battles for every rebound and loose ball... Strong defensively... Good shot blocker for a wing... Team player, but also knows when to take over... Phenomenal stamina
