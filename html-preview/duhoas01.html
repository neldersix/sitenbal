Scouting Report
There are times when the scoreboard wont keep up with him he scores so fast, other times however, it is easy to forget he is on the court... He sets up strong on the block and establishes position immediately and can seal off players with a simple use of his off arm... His free throw shooting must improve... Has the quickness to leave the paint and alter shots

Lacks great vision, passing skills... Needs to pound the glass this season to prove he can rebound... Lacks shot blocking ability
