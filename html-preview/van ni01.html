Has very quick hands, and reads the passing lanes, and can rip someone from the dibble... Generally impacts game tempo in a positive way when he's on the floor... Never appears tired, has great conditioning and work ethic

Lacks a 3 point shot and any real range on his shot... Not a big leaper
