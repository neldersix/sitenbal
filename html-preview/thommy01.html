Scouting Report
He sets up strong on the block and establishes position immediately and can seal off players with a simple use of his off arm... Free throw shooting is just average... Not a great passer, but decent... Quick off his feet and a good offensive rebounder... Has the quickness to leave the paint and alter shots... He is also a solid shoot-blocker... Unlike many players as athletic as he is, he doesn't rely on it he uses intelligence and skills instead

Not the greatest ball handling skills, but should be able to improve in this area
