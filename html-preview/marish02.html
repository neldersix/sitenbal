Scouting Report
Has shown the ability to create his own shot off the dribble and pulls up quickly and efficiently, squares to the basket very well and gets vertical... He has proven time and again that he can not only knock down shots from outside with consistency, but also big shots in big game stuations... Good free throw shooter, but should look to get to the line more often... Attacks the basket well with good ball handling ability... Really gets up and grabs rebounds... Has all the physical tools to be a shut-down defender... Good shot blocker for a wing... Phenomenal stamina

Needs to become a better passer to properly utilize his above average vision and his court awareness... Decision making can be questionable at times
