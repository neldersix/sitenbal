A prolific scorer who can really fill it up when he’s on... Gets to the line well and shoots at a solid clip... He has the uncanny ability to see passing lanes before they open up, thus enabling him to nearly always make the right decision with the ball... Good defender, team player. Has leadership ability... A real ball hawk... Knows how to create his own shot

His shot from outside needs major work, though it showed improvement... Mostly effective with the ball in his hands, so some feel he must dominate the ball to be effective
