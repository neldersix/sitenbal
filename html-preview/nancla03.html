Scouting Report
Very good at scoring in the low block, great touch... Has good touch on his shot, solid free throw shooter... Has excellent vision and passing ability for a player his size... Unlike most players his size, he can put the ball on the floor and shows tremendous court vision and a nice passing touch... Grabs rebounds well using his leaping ability... Excels at squaring up from 12 feet and taking bigger players off the dribble... Has a tremedous wingspan which allows him to block shots most players his size could only dream of... Makes good decisions and finds shots within the flow of the game, looking to get everyone involved and shooting when necessary
