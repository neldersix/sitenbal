Scouting Report
One of the purest shooters around... Excellent leaper, can receive the ball on the drive from the 3 point line and dunk the ball in traffic... His free throw shooting must improve... Solid vision and passing skills... Unlike most players his size, he can put the ball on the floor and shows tremendous court vision and a nice passing touch... He's not a huge leaper, but is a nice athlete that can get to the rim without any problem... Intimidating presence... Knows how to create his own shot
