Scouting Report
Fundamentally sound player with excellent size and shooting ability... He has great skills to let the ball go early against big men... Usually converts when he gets to the charity stripe... Has legitimate lead guard skills, with a yo yo handle, and solid passing ability... Really excels handling the ball... Gives solid effort defensively... Plays solid team defense, reads passing lanes for steals... In the open court he can lead the fastbreak or fill the lane... One of the most athletic players in all of college basketball

Has improved his rebounding but still plenty of room for improvement... Does not get great lift when he jumps
