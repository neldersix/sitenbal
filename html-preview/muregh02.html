Scouting Report
Feathery touch out to 12-15 feet... Free throw shooting showed significant improvement last year... A good jumper. He uses good positioning to grab a nice share of rebounds... Penetrates well with a good ability to attack the hoop, quick first step... Good shot blocker, with his long arms he should get a fair number of blocks... Good basketball understanding and decision maker... His physical strength is virtually unparalleled for a player his height

Could become a good shooter but probably never a great shooter... Lacks great passing and ball handling ability... Must get a lot better at protecting the ball
