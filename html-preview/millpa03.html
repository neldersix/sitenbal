Scouting Report
Very strong offensively, great form on his shot... Has a good power game inside and gets out on the break well... Solid free throw shooter... He can pass well if he is double teamed in the post... Unlike most players his size, he can put the ball on the floor and shows tremendous court vision and a nice passing touch... Excellent rebounder due in large part to his aggressiveness... Like a coach on the floor... One of the most athletic players in all of pro basketball
