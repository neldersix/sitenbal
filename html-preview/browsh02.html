Scouting Report
Excellent shot mechanics, but could use more arc on his shot... He has proven time and again that he can not only knock down shots from outside with consistency, but also big shots in big game stuations... Usually converts when he gets to the charity stripe... He has nice change of speed with the ball in his hands and very good ball handling making him dangerous with the dribble.... He best attribute is by far his defense

Needs to become a better passer to properly utilize his above average vision and his court awareness
