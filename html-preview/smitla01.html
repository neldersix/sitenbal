Scouting Report
He tends to over focus on his offense sometimes, creating situations where his teammates bring the ball up the court and look to him to score instead of playing as a team... Very good at scoring in the low block, great touch... Solid free throw shooter... One on one skills have improved along with his ball handling... Huge length and leaping ability make him an excellent rebounder... Plays a team game, and understands how to play, great court vision

Must become a better passer, especially out of the post... Not a defensive presence, lacks great explosiveness to block shots
