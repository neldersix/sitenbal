Scouting Report
His ability to create shots for himself off the dribble has improved... Spectauclar dunker, his explosiveness and quickness getting off the ground is unrivaled among college post players... Free throw shooting is just average... Has worked hard on cutting down turnovers as his improved A/TO ratio attests... A lane clogger, his immense strength makes things difficult for opposing players to operate inside... Has good body control and can even block some shots... Nice athlete, good decision maker, instinctual... One of the most athletic players in all of college basketball

Lacks the ability to improvise much in the open floor: catching and dribbling/passing before a shot, he's a lot more comfortable with set plays or crashing the glass
