Scouting Report
He is a natural scorer... He plays one-on-one very well, has nice moves around the basket where he usually finishes with power plays.... Good basketball disposition, doesn't get out of control or frustrated easily, makes good decisions passing the ball and has good vision... Has a huge wingspan... A good defender especially adept at blocking shots... He likes to run and the main thing is how fast he is able to change from defense to the break... A unique player because of his unreal body strength.

Can get sloppy handling the ball, leading to a few too many turnovers
