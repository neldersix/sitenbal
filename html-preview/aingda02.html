Scouting Report
Has good range and accuracy... Aggressive player who gets to the line often and converts his FTs at a good clip... Handles the ball very well in the open court with good speed... Has the versatility to guard on the perimeter and in the post... Has very quick hands, and reads the passing lanes, and can rip someone from the dibble... Competitive, good decision maker... Phenomenal stamina

He has problems defending stronger guys and playing post up on offense, has to improve his timing on rebounds and his patience on defense.... Lacks shot blocking ability
