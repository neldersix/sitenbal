Scouting Report
Very strong offensively, great form on his shot... He plays one-on-one very well, has nice moves around the basket where he usually finishes with power plays.... Free throw shooting still just average but showing steady improvement... He can pass well if he is double teamed in the post... Has decent ball handling ability... Crashes the boards especially hard, a tenacious rebounder on both ends of the floor... A tremendous lockdown defender... Good shot blocker for a wing... Good anticipation and understanding of body positioning... Truly a freak of nature in terms of athletic ability, agility and mobility, he simple has the ability to do anything and everything on the basketball court that he wants to do
