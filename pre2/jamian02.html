Clutch player who steps up and shows no conscience shooting the ball when the game is on the line... Extremely smooth post player... Great at drawing fouls and converting from the free throw line... One on one skills have improved along with his ball handling... An excellent offensive rebounder, leading to a lot of tip ins, and easy baskets... Huge wingspan allows him to play bigger than his height... He shows an interest in being a vocal leader on the court
